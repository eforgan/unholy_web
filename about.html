WHO WE ARE

Emerged from the underground in 2024. Born in darkness, raised by chaos. UNHOLY DEVOTION is a digital collective that transcends genres and defies conventions.

Based in the shadows of Buenos Aires, we are sound designers, visual artists, and ritual architects creating experiences that blur the line between reality and the digital abyss. Our philosophy is simple: no genres. No rules. Only devotion to the craft.

We have performed in the most exclusive underground venues across Europe and Asia, bringing our unique blend of industrial techno, dark ambient, and experimental noise to audiences worldwide. Every performance is a ritual, every track a prayer to the void.
