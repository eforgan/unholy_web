BIOGRAPHY

UNHOLY DEVOTION is a digital collective from Buenos Aires, Argentina, specializing in industrial techno, dark ambient, and experimental electronic music. Formed in 2024, the collective consists of sound designers, visual artists, and ritual architects who create immersive experiences that blur the line between reality and the digital abyss.

With a philosophy of "no genres, no rules," UNHOLY DEVOTION has performed in the most exclusive underground venues across Europe and Asia, including Berghain (Berlin), Fabric (London), Contact (Tokyo), and De School (Amsterdam).

Their discography includes the acclaimed "Genesis IV" EP (2024), the debut album "Unholy Devotion" (2024), and multiple singles and remixes. The collective has garnered a dedicated following of over 10,000 fans worldwide.
